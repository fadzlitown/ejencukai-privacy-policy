Your Privacy Matters. Ejen Cukai Scanner is designed with a privacy-first approach. Your financial data stays on your device and your personal Google Drive - we do not have access to your receipts or financial information.

1. Introduction

Inovasiti ("we," "us," or "our") operates Ejen Cukai Scanner ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

This policy is designed to comply with the Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA) of Malaysia and other applicable privacy regulations.

By using the App, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide Core Services: Enable receipt scanning, expense tracking, and report generation
  • Process Receipts with AI: Use Google Gemini AI to extract text from your receipt images
  • Enable Cloud Backup: Allow you to backup and restore your data via Google Drive
  • Improve the App: Analyze crash reports and usage patterns to fix bugs and enhance features
  • Communicate: Send important updates about the App (if you opt-in)

4. AI Processing and Data Handling

4.1 How AI Scanning Works

When you scan a receipt:

  1. Your receipt image is sent to Google Gemini AI for text extraction
  2. The AI processes the image and returns extracted text (merchant, amount, date, etc.)
  3. The extracted data is stored locally on your device
  4. If cloud backup is enabled, data is synced to your Google Drive

4.2 AI Data Retention

Google Gemini AI processes your images in real-time. According to Google's policies for AI services:

  • Images are processed transiently for text extraction
  • Google may retain data for service improvement (subject to Google's Privacy Policy)
  • For details, see Google's Privacy Policy

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Local Storage

  • Receipt data is stored in an encrypted SQLite database on your device
  • Images are stored in your device's secure app storage
  • Data is protected by your device's security features (PIN, fingerprint, etc.)

5.2 Cloud Storage (Google Drive)

  • If you enable cloud backup, data is stored in your personal Google Drive
  • We use Google's secure APIs to access only the App's designated folder
  • Your Google Drive data is protected by your Google account security
  • We cannot access your Google Drive data - only you can

5.3 Security Measures

  • Encrypted local database (SQLCipher)
  • Secure HTTPS connections for all data transfers
  • Firebase App Check to prevent unauthorized API access
  • OAuth 2.0 for Google authentication

Under Malaysia's Personal Data Protection Act 2010, you have the right to:

  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@ejencukai.com.

10. Data Retention

  • Local Data: Retained until you delete the App or clear App data
  • Google Drive Data: Retained until you delete it from your Google Drive
  • Crash Reports: Retained by Firebase for up to 90 days
  • Analytics Data: Retained in aggregated form for service improvement

11. Data Deletion

11.1 Deleting Local Data

  • Delete individual receipts within the App
  • Uninstall the App to remove all local data
  • Clear App data in your device settings

11.2 Deleting Cloud Data

  • Sign out from Google Drive in the App settings
  • Delete the "ReceiptKeeper" folder from your Google Drive
  • Revoke App access in your Google Account settings
